This is Steven Tomak's webpage for CSI 443 at Renton Technical College.

Steven has a long career in technology. He worked a Apple for 10 years in sales, support, and quality assurance. Now he works at Amazon as a QA Tester by day and takes night classes toward his BA.

In his free time, Steven loves guitar, chess, and long hikes with his wife Justine and his dog Eddie.

If you would like to challenge Steven to a game of chess, create an account on gameknot.com and send a challenge

Steven studied Physics at California Polytechnic State University starting in 2000. He conducted research on 10nm passivated silver nanocrystals created through vaproization and quenching.

After working at Apple and Amazon, Steven decided that application development was a better fit for a career path, so he went back to school at RTC.
